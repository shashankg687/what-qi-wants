Speaker Bio
Biman Chattopadhyay is the Co-founder and CTO of Quanfluence, a Bengaluru-based quantum computing startup building India's first general-purpose quantum computer using photonic technology. He leads the development of their optical Ising machine that processes hundreds of interconnected variables simultaneously for optimization problems. Quanfluence recently secured $2M in seed funding, and Biman holds patents in quantum random number generation. An entrepreneur at heart, he also co-founded SilabTech and is an active angel investor in deep-tech startups.
