Industry Challenge: Developing Quantum-Safe Security Frameworks

As the threat of quantum computers breaking traditional encryption grows, there is an urgent need for robust Post-Quantum Cryptography (PQC) and Quantum Key Distribution (QKD) systems. This problem statement challenges participants to explore novel algorithms or integration methods that ensure seamless transition to quantum-safe architectures in existing network infrastructures with minimal latency overhead.
